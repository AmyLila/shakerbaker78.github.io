East Idaho Weather

eastidahoweather.org

  • The main purpose of EastIdahoWeather.com is to educate viewers about the weather in Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven.

  • A secondary purpose is to highlight outdoor activities in the area.

  • Thirdly it will teach viewers simple, appropriate precautions to take while enjoying the outdoors in the predicted weather.
