Full-Time Missionary Accommodations

For couples who have chosen to dedicate some of their time to serving a full-time mission, we have accommodations. Our locations are close to the temple, so our lodgings are perfect for anyone who needs to go there regularly. We offer lodgings for as long as the call is for with 5 suites on every floor. Each suite has a bathroom, a bedroom, a living area, and a fully equipped kitchen. We hope these accommodations will keep you fit to serve.

Bathroom

Our missionary bathrooms have the regular accommodations of any bathroom along with some added bits of help. Depending on your preference the bathroom has a walk-in shower with a handrail and seat or a shower/bath mix.

Bedroom

Our suite bedroom comes furnished with a king-sized bed, two bedside tables on either side and a dresser. There is also a closet with built-in shelves and an area to hang clothing. Depending on your preference the mattress can be firm, medium, or soft.

Living Area

The living area comes furnished with a couch, two armchairs, and two side tables at the end of the couch. There are a coffee table and a TV/stand mix. The living room and the bedroom are carpeted.

Kitchen

Our kitchen features a hardwood floor along with our bathroom. It is fully equipped with a stove/oven mix, marble countertops, an island, a dining table, a sink, and cupboards. It also comes with 6 sets of dishes.
