Wedding Receptions

Marriage is a special event in anyone’s life, but sadly stress often accompanies the bride, groom, and anyone else planning and executing the event. Here at Temple Inn & Suites, we pride ourselves on helping your wedding go smoothly and efficiently. We have a ballroom, kitchens, staff, and special rooms for the bride and groom to use to prepare themselves with friends and family. We hope that with our help, you can have the wedding of your dreams.

Ballroom

Our ballroom is large enough for 50 dancing couples along with room for seating. With three crystal chandeliers, a polished marble floor, and white decorated walls it’ll feel like you’re waltzing into eternity.

Kitchen

Along with our state-of-the-art kitchens where you can prepare your dishes, we also have a series of set menu options available to make choosing your meals easy and efficient. Our chefs are also able to cater to different dietary needs.

Staff

Our staff is here to make your day go as smoothly as possible and can do a myriad of jobs. We have waiters and waitresses, cooks, and even a DJ. Our staff are competent and caring and will do what they can do make your special day effortless.

Preparation Rooms

Even before the wedding, we’re here to help. We have special rooms for the bride and the groom to prepare themselves with family and friends. Each room has space for 10 people and a closet where items can be placed before and after the wedding.
